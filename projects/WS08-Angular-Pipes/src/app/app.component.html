WS07 - Angular-Pipes

Built-in Pipes

DatePipe - Formats a date value according to locale rules.
Plain date: {{currentDate}}
With browsers-locale: {{currentDate | date}}
'yyyy. MMMM dd' - {{currentDate | date : 'yyyy. MMMM dd'}}
'yy-M-dd' - {{currentDate | date : 'yy-M-dd'}}
'yyyy.MM.dd HH:mm:ss' - {{currentDate | date : 'yyyy.MM.dd HH:mm:ss'}}
UpperCasePipe - Transforms text to all upper case.
{{'my text'| uppercase}}
LowerCasePipe - Transforms text to all lower case.
{{'MY TEXT'| lowercase}}
CurrencyPipe - Transforms a number to a currency string, formatted according to locale rules.
{{ 1000 | currency:'USD':'symbol'}}
{{ 1000 | currency:'FT':'symbol'}}
DecimalPipe - Transforms a number into a string with a decimal point, formatted according to locale rules.
{{1 | number:'3.5'}}
{{100 | number:'.2'}}
{{10000000 | number:'.2'}}
PercentPipe - Transforms a number to a percentage string, formatted according to locale rules.
{{ 0.36 | percent}}
{{ 0.25 | percent: '4.2'}}

Chaining pipes

Date TO long-format TO uppercase {{currentDate | date:'yyyy. MMMM dd' | uppercase}}
